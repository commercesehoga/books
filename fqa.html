Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about Boards Killer, Boards Dominator, and Thunderstudy's free CBSE Class 12 study materials.

What is Boards Killer?
Boards Killer is a free CBSE Class 12 study book by Thunderstudy for Humanities stream students. It covers History, Political Science, Geography, and English with PYQs, mind maps, topper answer sheets, and 5 sample papers. Download free at wondermayank.github.io.
What is Boards Dominator?
Boards Dominator is a free CBSE Class 12 study book by Thunderstudy for Science stream students. It covers Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Physical Education with PYQs, mind maps, topper sheets, and 5 sample papers.
Are Boards Killer and Boards Dominator really free?
Yes. Both books are 100% free. No signup, no payment. Download at wondermayank.github.io.
What is the difference between Boards Killer and Boards Dominator?
Boards Killer = Humanities (History, Pol. Sci, Geography, English). Boards Dominator = Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths, PE). Stream-specific, not difficulty-based.
Who created these books?
Wonder Mayank under the brand Thunderstudy (also known as Galaxy Official).
Is there a Commerce stream book?
Yes — Boards Pro Series covers Commerce (Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, English). Also free.
What is One Book For CUET?
An all-in-one CUET prep book at ₹99/subject (value ₹499). Includes 3000–5000+ MCQs, CBT mocks, PYQs, and mind maps.
Is there a free CBT mock test platform?
Yes. Practice free at commercesehoga.github.io/dashboard — full-length timed tests for CBSE and CUET.
